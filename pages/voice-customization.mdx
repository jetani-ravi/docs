## How to Add voice in Your Voice Nova Agent.

Voice Nova allows you to fully customize your agent's speaking voice. You can choose from a wide range of voice models, languages, genders, and providers to match your use case.

###  What is the Voice Library?

The **Voice Library** is a central place to explore and manage available voices for your agents. You can:

- Browse all available voice models
- Filter by **language**, **gender**, and **provider**
- Add or remove voices to personalize your agent's speaking style

## <Icon icon="circle-check" size={27} /> Next Steps

Now that you’ve set up voices for your agent, here’s what you can do next:

- **Fine-tune Prompts**: Adjust your agent’s prompt to better match the tone of the selected voice.
- **Test More Voices**: Experiment with different providers and languages to see what fits best.
- **Go Live**: Use your agent in real scenarios and get user feedback on the voice experience.

## Tips for Better Voice Customization

<Tip>
  - Use **short, clear phrases** in your agent’s prompt to match natural-sounding voice output.
  - Choose voices that match your **target audience's** language and tone preferences.
  - Always **test** the voice after adding it to ensure clarity and tone are appropriate.
  - Check provider documentation for **voice limits or pricing differences**.
</Tip>
